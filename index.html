What is EnergyAtlas.io?

EnergyAtlas.io is an urban-scale simulation platform that transforms cities into data-rich digital twins. Built on years of research in Urban Building Energy Modeling (UBEM), it enables users to simulate energy use, analyze decarbonization pathways, and prioritize retrofits city-wide.

Key Features

  • Urban Digital Twin Builder: Generate 3D building models using open and proprietary geospatial data.
  • City-Scale Energy Simulation: Run operational energy use and emissions simulations across building stocks.
  • Scenario Planning: Evaluate electrification, envelope upgrades, and renewables integration.
  • Retrofit Prioritization: Identify cost-effective upgrades using building archetypes and emissions profiles.
  • Public Dashboard: Communicate results through interactive maps and charts.
  • Integration-Ready: Compatible with EnergyPlus, OpenStudio, and external dashboards.

Use Cases

  • Municipal climate action planning
  • Resilience assessment under extreme weather
  • Policy simulations and compliance tracking
  • Equity mapping for energy interventions
  • Academic and policy research

Backed by Research

EnergyAtlas.io is developed at Cornell University's Environmental Systems Lab and has been successfully applied in Ithaca, NYC, and Tompkins County to guide decarbonization and retrofit efforts.
